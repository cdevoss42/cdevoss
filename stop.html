stop time


in twilight i dreamed I had found the spark of me, the lesser of you, the greater of my grandpapa, and the line of love between men.

in daytime I saw the truth of what i was trying to do, the brighter side of you, the greater of my reflection day self, and the line of lies between men.

at dusk, I envisoned the dark side of my desires, the greater love of my true friends, and the stack of support I got when I really did not deserve it.

at bedtime, I realized I had not the need to be trying so hard., the loads of egos i encountered, and the pile of untrue friends. i thought were true. they only used me.. as I used myself to prop myself on the piano, and pretend to play, or jump in the middle of the dance floor. only to slip and bust my lip…

at death………………………. who knows… I am not dead.

so in life, I dream of friends that are true, of days that are bright, of nights where the demons of the past stay at bay. I leave my ears open, I capture my heart before it closes between the soul. My eyes are still open, my touch for love reaches, my pain in my eyes are real.

never let one day go, without a closure of happy, or a locked up of pain. life……………… does not give away the minutes… you take them up with worry……. stop.

f.messina 11.27.23
